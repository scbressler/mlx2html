Square Wave Builder

Fourier Series Visualization
The square wave is a special waveform common in electronics and signal processing. In music, it's one of the fundamental oscillators used in subtractive synthesis.
A square wave is built from the sum of infinitely many odd-integer harmonics — scaled sinusoids at frequencies f, 3f, 5f, 7f… This is Fourier decomposition in action.
Use the sliders to set the fundamental frequency and number of harmonics. Watch the square wave take shape as harmonics accumulate. Switch to Frequency Domain to see the spectral content. Hit Plot It to render.
